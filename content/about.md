# About this gallery

Welcome to the OST Gallery, which showcases of images of the deep-sky and solar system objects captured with telescopes at the observatory of the University of Potsdam. These images were taken by students and/or staff from the Astrophysics Department, either as part of the astrophysics lab courses or in their spare time. 

## What you will find here

- Images organised by object type: galaxies, nebulae, star clusters, the solar system, and more
- Observation details including filters, exposure notes, and the observer who took each image
- Larger views and background information for many captures

## The observatory

Detailed information about our telescope and instruments can be found in the <a href="https://polaris.astro.physik.uni-potsdam.de/wiki">wiki</a>.
